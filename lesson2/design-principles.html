Visual Hierarchy

RCOE

https://rcoe.us/

Visual Hierarchy is incredibly important in website design, and assists in helping users notice immediately important information on a web page. Careful placement of elements on a webpage creates a sense of purpose and direction for your theme/product. Also, helps your user quickly navigate to their desired information. I feel like RCOE displayed very good Visual Hierarchy with their text and color, making it very easy to know where to navigate on the page

White Space and
Clean Design

accuquilt

https://www.accuquilt.com

White Space and Clean Design makes for and articulate and meaningful website design. Using white space evenly helps make your content easily scannable and improves significantly readability. Effective use of white space assists user in understanding your message quickly and concisely. Additionally, white space helps users to make logical sense of the information presented to them. I felt like accuquilt did a nice job implementing White Space, which in turn helps visitors navigate much quicker through the website

PARC: Alignment

Target

https://www.target.com

Alignment is important for making a very visually appealing website design. Alignment allows an observer to read the content more easily and clearly. This process is achieved by aligning your elements in such a way to establish order and avoid random positioning. Alignment like other design principle helps establish order in your website design. I thought Target did a great job with Alignment. Their website has a very fluid and appealing design, which intices the users to navigate to multiple areas with ease.
